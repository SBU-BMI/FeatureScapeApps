RadPath – FeatureScape Interactive Tool to Allow Feature Exploration Across Multiple Molecular, Radiology and Pathology Dimensions

Morphology features extracted from image analysis of histology whole slide images for 32 Lung Adenocarcinoma and Squamous Cell Carcinoma Patients of The Cancer Genome Atlas. Genomic data compiled with MSKCC cBioPortal tool, demographic and clinical data from LUAD and LUSC in the TCGA repository and preliminary Radiology data from The Cancer Imaging Archive.
